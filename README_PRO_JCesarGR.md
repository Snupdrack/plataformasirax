## 🚀 Latest Build: SIRAX

<div align="center">

### 🛡️ SIRAX — Identity & Risk Intelligence Platform

**Know More. Risk Less.**

</div>

SIRAX is an identity verification and risk intelligence platform focused on **KYC, compliance, digital footprint analysis, OSINT enrichment and risk scoring for Mexico-focused workflows**.

It centralizes identity validation, provider orchestration, digital footprint enrichment, compliance screening, risk scoring and structured reporting into a single operational workflow.

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%">
      <h3>🔎 What SIRAX Solves</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Fragmented identity verification processes</li>
        <li>Manual provider checks across disconnected systems</li>
        <li>Unstructured OSINT and digital footprint signals</li>
        <li>Risk data that is hard to normalize and explain</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
    <td width="50%">
      <h3>⚙️ Core Capabilities</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>CURP, RFC, RENAPO and SAT-oriented validation</li>
        <li>Email, phone and username enrichment</li>
        <li>SerpAPI, Sherlock and Maigret-style OSINT workflows</li>
        <li>Provider fallbacks, status checks and report generation</li>
      </ul>
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>
